Role-Based Query Set
Date: 2025-10-26

Notes:
- SQL queries are designed for roles with database access to hr_data (C-Level, HR).
- RAG queries reference available documents (employee_handbook.md, engineering_master_doc.md, financial_summary.md, quarterly_financial_report.md, marketing reports, compliance_sample.md).
- Use these as ready-made prompts in the chatbot by logging in as the respective role.

================================================================================
Role: C-Level (SQL + RAG)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SQL (5):
1) Show total employees by department, sorted by highest count first.
2) List the top 10 employees with performance_rating >= 4, including name, department, location, and salary.
3) What is the average salary for each department? Order the results by average salary descending.
4) Show employees hired in 2024 with performance_rating >= 4, including name, department, and hire_date.
5) Count employees per location and department (group by location, department) and order by count descending.

RAG (5):
1) Provide an executive summary of the quarterly financial report highlighting key risks and opportunities.
3) Identify the top compliance risks and recommended mitigations from the compliance document.
4) Give a cross-department highlights summary using marketing_report_2024.md and financial_summary.md.
5) Provide an executive summary of the engineering master document, focusing on architecture and delivery practices.

Role: HR (SQL + RAG)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SQL (5):
1) List all employees in the HR department with columns: name, role, location, performance_rating.
3) How many employees were hired in 2023?
4) What is the average salary in the Data department?
5) List employees located in Mumbai with performance_rating >= 4, showing name, department, and salary.

RAG (5):
1) Explain the company's leave policy in simple terms.
2) Outline the recruitment process from the employee handbook.
3) Summarize employee benefits available to full-time employees.
4) Provide performance appraisal guidelines and best practices for managers.
5) List the official company holidays and any rules around optional holidays.

Role: Finance (RAG)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
RAG (5):
1) Summarize the Quarterly Financial Report with key metrics and notable changes.
2) Provide a concise overview of the Financial Summary, including revenue and expense highlights.
3) Explain the revenue and cost breakdown and any trends mentioned in the financial documents.
4) Identify key financial risks and controls referenced across the financial and compliance documents.
5) Provide a budget allocation overview by department, based on the financial documents.
